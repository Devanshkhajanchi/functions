Coffee Recipes

Below are some recipes you can learn. Nothing is exact, but it gets the work done.

Espresso

An Espresso is just an Espresso. Pretty Self Explanatory.

Doppio

A Doppio is just an espresso but twice.

Cafe Zorro

Basically an espresso with an equal amount of water, because your weak soul can’t handle espressos.

Americano

An Americano is just an Espresso shot, but you added water to it to feel better about yourself. So American.

Cortado

A Cortado is equal parts Espresso and steamed milk. usually at least an ounce each.

Flat White

Flat Whites are just Cortados with more milk. Twice as much milk as espresso to be exact.

Cafe Latte

Lattes are Flat Whites with even more steamed milk, topped with some milk foam.

Cappuccino

Cappuccinos are Lattes with less steamed milk, topped with some milk foam. Usually has a dusting of chocolate powder.

Cafe Mocha

A Mocha is essentially a Latte, but with chocolate syrup or cocoa powder mixed into the drink. Typically topped with whipped cream.

Affogato

An Affogato is a scoop of vanilla gelato (Or ice cream for you uncultured folks) “drowned” in a shot of hot espresso.

Red Eye

A Red Eye is an espresso shot added to twice the amnount of drip coffee.

Black Eye

A Black Eye is like the Red Eye, but with two shots of espresso added to the drip coffee.

Hot Chocolate

This isn’t even coffee, but some people might get mad if it weren’t here, so here it is.
