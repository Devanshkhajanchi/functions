Coffee Recipes

Below are some recipes you can learn. Nothing is exact, but it gets the work done.

Espresso

An Espresso is just an Espresso. Pretty Self Explanatory.

Doppio

A Doppio is just an espresso but twice.

Cafe Zorro

Basically an espresso with an equal amount of water, because your weak soul can’t handle espressos.

Americano

An Americano is just an Espresso shot, but you added water to it to feel better about yourself. So American.

Cortado

A Cortado is equal parts Espresso and steamed milk. usually at least an ounce each.
